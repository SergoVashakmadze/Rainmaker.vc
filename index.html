Our Investment Philosophy

Rainmaker VC partners with visionary founders who are building technologies that have the potential to fundamentally change how the world works. We are a venture capital fund with a technology-first, sector-agnostic approach and global investment scope.

Innovation-First

We invest in companies pushing the boundaries of what is possible, leveraging breakthrough technologies and novel approaches to solve meaningful problems at scale.

Disruptive Potential

We seek businesses that challenge incumbents and create new market categories through superior products, business models, or go-to-market strategies.

Scalability-Driven

We focus on ventures with capital-efficient unit economics, strong network effects, and the ability to rapidly expand across markets and customer segments.

Early-Stage Investment Focus

We invest in the earliest stages of a company's journey, from initial concept to Series A.

  • Pre-Seed ($100K – $500K): Idea validation and MVP development for pre-revenue startups.
  • Seed ($500K – $2M): Product-market fit validation for companies with early traction to $500K ARR.
  • Series A ($2M – $10M): Go-to-market acceleration for companies with $500K – $3M ARR.

What We Look For

Exceptional Founders

Obsessive problem-solvers with deep domain expertise, proven ability to attract world-class talent, strong technical or go-to-market backgrounds, and resilience through market cycles.

Large Market Opportunity

Addressable markets of $1B+ with clear expansion paths, secular tailwinds driving long-term growth, and global scalability.

Defensible Technology

Proprietary technology with 10x better performance, network effects, data advantages, patent protection or unique IP assets.

Strong Unit Economics

Clear path to profitability with improving margins, capital-efficient customer acquisition, high LTV/CAC ratios greater than 3x.

How We Support Our Portfolio

  • Network Access: Introductions to customers, partners, and follow-on investors across our extensive global network.
  • Talent Acquisition: Help recruiting executive leadership and building world-class teams.
  • Go-to-Market: Strategic support on pricing, positioning, channel strategy, and customer acquisition.
  • Global Expansion: Guidance on international market entry, local partnerships, and cross-border operations.
  • Follow-On Capital: Continued funding in subsequent rounds and facilitation of syndicate partnerships.
  • Strategic Guidance: Board-level advice on strategy, product roadmap, fundraising, and exit opportunities.

Why Rainmaker VC

Positioned at the forefront of the technology revolution with global reach, a sector-agnostic approach, deep technology expertise, and hands-on operational support for portfolio companies.
